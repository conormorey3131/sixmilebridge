Our Community

Discover the heart of Sixmilebridge - our people, organizations, and shared spirit

Community Highlights

Active GAA Club

Our award-winning hurling and football teams bring the community together through sport, with teams for all ages and regular community events at our excellent facilities.

Vibrant Volunteering

Over 200 active volunteers contribute to Tidy Towns, community events, local charities, and neighborhood support - making Sixmilebridge a caring place to live.

Strong Schools Community

Our primary and secondary schools serve as community hubs, bringing families together through education, events, and shared activities.

Community Spirit

The true strength of Sixmilebridge lies in its people. Our community of approximately 2,500 residents comes together through shared values, mutual support, and a genuine care for one another. Whether you're a lifelong resident or new to our town, you'll find a warm welcome and opportunities to get involved.

From our bustling community center to the local GAA grounds, from traditional music sessions to volunteer initiatives, Sixmilebridge thrives because its residents actively participate in making it a better place for everyone.

What Makes Us Special

Our community is built on the foundation of Irish hospitality and neighborly support. We celebrate together during festivals and sporting victories, and we rally around each other during challenging times. This spirit of togetherness is what makes Sixmilebridge more than just a place to live - it's a place to belong.

The blend of young families, established residents, and seniors creates a rich tapestry of experiences and wisdom that strengthens our entire community.

Community Facilities

Community Center

Our modern community center hosts meetings, classes, celebrations, and serves as the social heart of our town with something happening most days of the week.

GAA Grounds

Top-class sporting facilities that host matches, training sessions, and community events. The clubhouse is also available for private functions and meetings.

Playground & Parks

Safe, well-maintained spaces for children and families to play and relax, plus walking trails along the beautiful O'Garney River.

Local Businesses

Our shops, pubs, and services aren't just businesses - they're community gathering places where neighbors meet, chat, and support each other.
