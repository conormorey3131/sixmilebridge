Sixmilebridge, a small town doing big things.

Built on heritage, industry, community and momentum, this is a place where ideas are the birth of realities. Close to Shannon, in the County of Clare, on the western side of Ireland, and increasingly impossible to ignore. Sixmilebridge is where long-established roots meet fast-moving ambition.

Powered by the Sixmilebridge Town Team

This website exists because locals backed locals to build a better town. The Sixmilebridge Town Team is a volunteer-led community group working on behalf of the town. Their focus is to implement actions of the Town Centre First Plan in a practical, measured and impact-driven manner. Priorities are to:

  • Champion the community and the people who power it
  • Support investment, enterprise and local projects
  • Promote events, organisations, clubs and businesses
  • Protect what makes Sixmilebridge unique while pushing for progress
  • Create a town presence that reflects reality
  • Achieve steady work and tangible outcomes

Why this site exists

To put Sixmilebridge firmly on the map as a:

  • A central hub for residents, businesses and visitors
  • A spotlight for local initiatives and upcoming events
  • A place to back local enterprise and civic pride
  • A starting point for anyone investing, visiting or getting involved
  • Proof that a community with momentum can build its own story

A website built and funded locally, for everyone.

This is a town that shows up for itself. Clubs, schools, businesses, employers, volunteers, planners, builders, organisers, doers.

What you see here is a step forward, powered by people who care about where this place goes next.

Because the best towns don't happen by accident. They happen by design, effort and occasional stubbornness. And Sixmilebridge has all three in healthy supply.

Sponsored by St. Francis Credit Union Limited

This Sixmilebridge website is sponsored by the local community based branch of St. Francis Credit Union Limited, in the heart of the town of Sixmilebridge.

The St. Francis Credit Union serves the community by providing:

  • Financial services to the local residents and businesses
  • Loans and savings to the community
  • Support for a large range of Community organisations and events

In 2024, Sixmilebridge Town Team received a grant award from the St. Francis Credit Union Limited, Social and Cultural Fund. Funding had been sought to develop branding and a website for the town. This award delivered a key goal on the Sixmilebridge Town Centre First Plan. It will deliver improved communication with the community, showcase the businesses and activities in the town and highlight what the town has to offer the visiting tourist.

The Sixmilebridge Town Team is delighted to receive this sponsorship from the local branch of St. Francis Credit Union Limited, located in the heart of the town.
