A small town doing big things.

Sixmilebridge has always punched above its size. Built on heritage, industry, community and momentum, this is a place where ideas don’t gather dust. They happen. Close to Shannon, connected to Clare, and increasingly impossible to ignore, Sixmilebridge is where long-established roots meet fast-moving ambition.

Powered by the Sixmilebridge Town Team

This website exists because locals backed locals to build something better. The Sixmilebridge Town Team is a volunteer-led community group working on behalf of the town. Their focus is practical, measured and impact-driven:

  • Champion the community and the people who power it
  • Support investment, enterprise and local projects
  • Promote events, organisations, clubs and businesses
  • Protect what makes Sixmilebridge unique while pushing for progress
  • Create a town presence that reflects reality, not nostalgia

No slogans. No spin. Just steady work and tangible outcomes.

Why this site exists

Simply to put Sixmilebridge firmly on the map for the right reasons.

  • A central hub for residents, businesses and visitors
  • A spotlight for local initiatives and upcoming events
  • A place to back local enterprise and civic pride
  • A starting point for anyone investing, visiting or getting involved
  • Proof that a community with momentum can build its own narrative

Built locally. Funded locally. For everyone.

This is a town that shows up for itself. Clubs, schools, businesses, employers, volunteers, planners, builders, organisers, doers. The sum total is greater than the population count ever suggests.

What you see here is another step forward, powered by people who care about where this place goes next.

Because the best towns don’t happen by accident. They happen by design, effort and occasional stubbornness. And Sixmilebridge has all three in healthy supply.
